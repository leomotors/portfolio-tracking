# Product

## Register

product

## Users

The primary user is the portfolio owner reviewing investments, bank balances, credit lines, allocation, and daily net worth history in an authenticated dashboard.

## Product Purpose

Portfolio Tracking consolidates personal financial positions, updates prices and balances, and presents allocation and performance views that make day-to-day portfolio review easier.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and utilitarian. The dashboard should feel trustworthy and easy to scan, with design choices serving financial clarity over decoration.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page composition, oversized hero treatments, decorative chart chrome, loud fintech gradients, and UI patterns that make dense financial data harder to compare.

## Design Principles

- Prioritize scanability for repeated portfolio review.
- Keep financial numbers and category relationships visually clear.
- Use restrained product UI patterns that fit the existing dashboard shell.
- Preserve consistency across overview, investments, and allocation surfaces.
- Prefer accessible color and readable contrast over ornamental styling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve reduced-motion support for any animation and avoid making color the only way to understand chart categories.
